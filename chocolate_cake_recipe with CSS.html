Recipe

Chocolate Cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!"

`
Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80 g

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate together with the butter

2

Mix sugar, egs and flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake at 180 C for 50 minutes
